inContext

Freedom of identity and control over privacy are the top priorities.

inContext is part of a class project for CMS.100 at MIT, taught by Professor Fox Harrell. The aim of inContext was to redefine the Facebook profile in a way that empowered users to determine their identities and give them total control over what they share. This is the solution I came up with, made in a six day rush while working on other final projects. Hope you find it interesting!
